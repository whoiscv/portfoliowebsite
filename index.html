About Me

As a creative, I believe in the power of visual storytelling to create impactful, layered images that form a narrative on their own or in a series. This is something I aspire to with all of my work, whether I'm on assignment or exploring personal projects.
Community and conservation also figure large in my field. As we learn more about how large-scale industrial activities and social forces affect the world we live in, I've been lucky to witness how the solutions to some of our planet's biggest challenges can arise from local, grassroots initiatives.
